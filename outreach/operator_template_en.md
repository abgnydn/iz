## What "personalize each one" means

Before sending, look up the operator's most recent disclosure and reference it specifically. Example:

> For Akçansa, the bench currently shows Büyükçekmece 1,514k tCO₂ (from your 2025 IAR p46 × p167 clinker share), Çanakkale 3,466k, Ladik 499k. Total group: 5.48M. I'd like to confirm these against your records.

Personalization signals you've actually read their report and aren't blasting a generic.

## What to do with the response

- **They share a number that matches ours within ±10%:** thank them, mark the row with their reference, no further work.
- **They share a number that differs >10%:** update the bench, note the reason for the difference, request methodology details (EF used, captive power treatment, allocation key).
- **They decline / no response in 2 weeks:** mark as "outreach attempted N/A". Move on.

---

## Tone notes

- Lead with the public-good framing, not "I can save you money." Sustainability directors
  see vendor pitches daily; the open-source angle stands out.
- The €2B/yr industry-wide impact is for the press kit, not the cold email. Don't
  oversell at the individual operator level.
- Be willing to be wrong. If they correct a value, that's a win.
- Never imply the operator is "overpaying" — they may already be filing accurate data
  through their verifier. The bench just makes the publicly-derivable number easier to find.
