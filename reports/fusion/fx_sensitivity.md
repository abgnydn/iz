## Reading the table

- TR cement exporters face CBAM at **2-5% of revenue** under EU default — material to margin (typical cement EBITDA ~20%).

- Cement operators save **1-3% of revenue** by submitting MRV — full benefit goes directly to bottom line.

- BF/BOF integrated steel (Erdemir, Kardemir) has the highest absolute CBAM bill but lower revenue-share savings because EU default EF ≈ TR actual.

- FX appreciation (TL/EUR → higher) increases the revenue-denominated burden of CBAM (since bill is in EUR, revenue in TL). At TL/EUR = 60, cement makers face ~4-5% revenue erosion at EU default.

- FX depreciation (TL/EUR → lower toward 30) provides relief but the structural CBAM-savings gap (default vs actual) remains constant in EUR terms.
