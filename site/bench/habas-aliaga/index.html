04 Satellite cross-check

The TROPOMI flux-divergence catalog of Beirle et al. (2023, ESSD) lists a NOx source within 3.1 km of this plant:

0.344 ± 0.059 kg NOx/s (colocated source: Enka İzmir Doğalgaz Santrali;İzdemir Enerji power station)

NOx is an activity proxy independent of operator disclosure. A catalog hit within 15 km means the plant (or its colocated captive power) is bright enough in TROPOMI to register globally. This row is in the data/beirle_match_audit_grade.csv join shipped with the bench.
