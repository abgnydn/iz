# Co-signer outreach — TR-MRV-Bench position paper

**Goal:** get industry associations + operators to co-sign the CBAM position paper
(https://iz-mrv.pages.dev/policy/ · DOI 10.5281/zenodo.20496086) as a permanent,
citable artifact for the December 2027 default-values revision and the upcoming
Draft Act feedback phase of EU Initiative 14828.

**No false deadline.** Earlier drafts referenced a "10 June 2026" consultation
that does not exist — the Initiative 14828 Call for Evidence closed 25 Sep 2025
and the Draft Act feedback phase is "Upcoming." Co-signers join a permanent
position paper, not a deadline race.

**Core framing (the differentiation):** TÜRKÇİMENTO has publicly argued since
March 2026 that the EU should recognize national MRV data instead of the
"Other Countries" default. This paper is the *empirical infrastructure* for that
argument — 21 audit-grade facilities, 10,691 EUTL-verified plant-years of
cross-validation, an independently rediscovered formula, all open-source.
